About

Do you have a hard time finding accurate, reliable, and honest sources on a topic you know little about? Finding good content is difficult, especially when you’re setting out to learn something new by yourself. Unacademic is an open-source platform for collecting and sharing learning resources.

Search for people you want to learn from, or find your own articles, videos and podcasts on a topic. Add them to an existing list or create your own. You can learn - or teach - what you want, when you want, where you want with the support of a community of people just like you. Learn differently.

Curating the Web

How many wheels have you had to reinvent already? Our guess would be ‘too many’. Somehow, we keep creating new textbooks and courses, but most information is out there already. You just need a set up that organizes the information in one place, with just the right amount of structure. You don’t want to be told what to learn, but you don’t want to have to figure out everything yourself either. So gather those interesting blog posts, podcasts, or tutorials, add your comments, and share them. That’s it!

3 Modes

Learn more effectively by switching between three modes – learning, curating, and browsing. Be on the constant lookout for the clearest best resources for you learning needs. Changing your perspective makes your learning more active, and your retention will surge. Because you’re switching from the big picture to the small picture and back again, your understanding of a topic –from starting your own vineyard, to creating an awesome platform game– will be impressive.

Distributed Versions

This one’s a bit technical. Say, you decide to work through a set of learning resources on Unacademic. When you click start, you make a copy of that list. The new list is entirely yours. Make your own additions or changes. The person who curated the original list will get a notification, and can decide to include your changes or stick to their own version. Anyone else can do the same with your list. All the copies build up like a tree with interactive branches, and so the learning content stays alive and up to date.

Progress tracking

Sharing and comparing your progress with others is a proven way to keep you motivated. But if you’re not competitive, it’s still good to know that you’re not alone. Unacademic allows you to track your progress and see how you measure up against your peers. Whether you’re in it for the simple joy of learning or to build your presence in a community, it will help you stay motivated and stimulate you to reach just a little bit further.

Rate and Rank

When you’re new to a topic, the amount of learning resources (even when they’re curated) can be overwhelming. It can help you a lot to see how many people already copied a list and the number of stars they gave it. And when you’ve curated a list, every star is a sign of appreciation. With enough stars, you’ll be an authority on the topic and of great value to your community.

Community

In school, the teacher decides what you should learn. Here, that’s you! You gather the most relevant learning resources on any topic and learn or teach whatever you want, with the input and support of a community of people just like you. Get in touch with people from all over the world who are interested in the same things as you. Imagine the different perspectives and what you’ll learn from them.
